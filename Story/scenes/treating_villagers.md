# Scene: Triage at the Chapel

## Scene
triage_chapel

## Entry Condition
Player chose: Ask about the sick villagers

## Location
Village chapel

## Narrative
Candles flicker against stone walls.
Villagers lie on straw mats, coughing and feverish.
You quickly assess their symptoms.

Without treatment, many will not survive the night.

## Gameplay Focus
- Introduces villager severity system
- Introduces urgency and time pressure

## Player Choices
1. Treat the most critical villager first
2. Treat a moderate case first
3. Delay treatment and gather ingredients

## Branches
- Choice 1 → treatment_scene (high risk, high reward)
- Choice 2 → treatment_scene (moderate risk)
- Choice 3 → forest_gathering OR village_gathering
