# Scene: Intro / Arrival

## Scene ID
intro_arrival

## Location
Village entrance

## Narrative
Year 1349. The Black Plague spreads across the land.
You arrive at a dying medieval village.
The streets are quiet, doors boarded shut.

A villager approaches, pale and shaking.

Villager:
"Doctor… thank the heavens you have arrived.
Our people are sick. Some are dying.
Rats roam our streets at night.
If you cannot save us… no one will."

## Player Choices
1. Ask about the sick villagers
2. Ask about the rats
3. Inspect your medical supplies

## Branches
- Choice 1 → triage_chapel
- Choice 2 → rat_warning OR rat_encounter
- Choice 3 → forest_gathering OR village_gathering
