#ODDS OF SURVIVAL – Storyline
Overview: a visual novel game set during the Black Plague in the year 1349. The player takes on the role of a traveling plague doctor who arrives in a dying medieval village. Rats spread disease through the streets, villagers fall ill, and time is limited. The player must gather ingredients, brew potions, and decide who to treat, knowing that every choice affects survival outcomes.
Characters: Plague Doctor, Villagers, Rats
Goal: The player’s goal is to gather ingredients and create potions to cure as many villagers as possible while avoiding infection from rats. Survival is not guaranteed, and difficult choices must be made throughout the game.
Probability/Choices: Throughout the visual novel, the player is presented with three to four choices per scenario.
These choices affect:
•    Villager survival
•    Player infection risk
•    Overall odds of survival
All outcomes are influenced by a hidden survival calculation system.
Ingredients: (some may be found in the forest, others more in the village)
Silverleaf, yarrow, thyme, mint leaves, bitter mushroom, mold bark, hawthorn berries, charcoal powder, bone ash, garlic bulbs
Potions: healing tonic – silverleaf, yarrow, mint leaves; Fever Suppressant – thyme sprigs, hawthorn berries, mint leaves; Plague Concoction – bitter mushroom, molded bark, charcoal powder; Ash Remedy – bone ash, garlic bulbs, charcoal powder, Elixir – silver leaf, garlic bulbs, charcoal 
(5 potions, 10 ingredients)

##RAT ENCOUNTER
Story: Suddenly — rats burst from the shadows!
Player Choice:
1.    Run away
2.    Attempt to scare them
3.    Stand still and observe
Rat Outcome - 
•    Running → Moderate infection risk
•    Scaring → Low risk
•    Standing still → High risk
Story: (if infected): You feel sharp pain. A bite. Your chances of survival have decreased.

##POTION BREWING
