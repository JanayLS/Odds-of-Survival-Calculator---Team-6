# Plague Village
## Intro - Arrival
**Setting:** Year 1349. The Black Plague spreads across the land, leaving fear, death, and silence in its wake. You are a traveling plague doctor, trained in medicine, and survival. Your journey has brought you to a remote village rumored to be on the brink of collapse.

### Dialogue
**Villager:** Doctor...thank the Heavens you have arrived. Our people are sick. Some are dying. Rats roam our streets at night. If you cannot save us...no one will!

### Player Choice 1
**What do you do first?**
1. Ask about the sick villagers
2. Ask about the rats
3. Inspect your medical supplies
